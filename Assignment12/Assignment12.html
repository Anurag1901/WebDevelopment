About :

To describe someone's height, you can say that they are tall or short. Someone who is thin and tall can be called lanky.To say someone is short and also small, you can say that they are petite. If you're talking about a child, they might be pint-sized.

Someone who weighs more than average can be curvy, well-built,full-bodied or heavy. (Curvy is usually only used to describe women). You might also say that someone has some meat on thier bones.This is a casual way of saying that they are overweight, and it might seem rude to some sensitive people. The opposite of overweight can be thin,skinny or slim.

Light, yellowish hair can be described as blonde.But you can also call a person who as that kind of hair a blonde.(This means that blonde can be used as a noun or adjective). A person with dark hair can be called a brunette.Someone with red hair can be called a redhead.

Besides what colour someone's hair is, you can say that they are short-haired or long-haired .You can say that they have ciurly, straight or wavy hair. Sometimes hair can also be frizzy, which is when it looks puffy with small,tight curls. If someone has no hair at all on their head, they are bald.

If someone dresses well, They are smartly dressed or they look smart. You can also just say that they are well-dressed. If someone dresses fashionably, they are stylish or trendy. If someone has bad taste in clothes, you can say they're unfashionable.A less nice way to say this is frumpy.

Many people have mannerism that appear when they're feeling negative(bad) emotions.If someone feels nervous or impatient, they might tap their fingers, crack their knuckles, bite thier fingernails or chew the tips of their pencils.
